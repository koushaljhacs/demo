About Koshiv Technology and Logistics

Building a modern logistics company with 40+ years of family transport experience and fresh technology perspective.

Our Story

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd was founded by a 3rd year B.Tech Computer Science student who grew up in a family with deep roots in the transport industry. Watching his father, grandfather, and other family members navigate the logistics world for decades, he saw both the strengths and the gaps.

The transport industry in India is largely unorganized, with outdated processes, lack of transparency, and minimal technology adoption. Yet, it's the backbone of our economy.

Koshiv is our attempt to bridge this gap - combining four generations of family transport experience with modern technology. We're not claiming to have all the answers yet. We're in the process of building, learning, and preparing for our 2026 launch.

Mission & Values

The principles that guide how we build Koshiv.

Our Mission

To bring transparency, efficiency, and technology to Indian logistics while honoring the hard-earned wisdom of family transport veterans.

Learn More →

Our Vision

To build a trusted logistics partner that clients and drivers both prefer - through honest pricing, real-time visibility, and respect for everyone in the supply chain.

Learn More →

Our Approach

Start small, be honest about our journey, learn from every client and partner, and grow sustainably without overpromising.

Learn More →

Core Values

Honesty

We don't pretend to be something we're not. We're transparent about our current status, our plans, and our limitations.

Respect for Experience

We honor the 40+ years of transport wisdom in our family. Technology should serve experience, not replace it.

Customer First

We build with the customer's real needs in mind - transparency, reliability, and clear communication.

Driver Respect

Drivers are the backbone of logistics. We're building policies and technology that respect their time and effort.

Sustainable Growth

We're starting small, learning from every interaction, and growing only when we're ready.

Technology with Purpose

Every feature we build must solve a real problem, not just look impressive.
