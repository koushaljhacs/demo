FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our launch

When will Koshiv actually start moving freight?
We're on track for Q2 2026 launch. Our first trucks arrive in April, with client shipments beginning in May. Join our early adopter list to be first in line and get priority capacity when we launch.
Can I book shipments now for 2026?
Yes! We're accepting pre-bookings with no obligation. Reserve your capacity today and lock in early adopter pricing (10% off first 5 shipments). You can cancel anytime before our official launch.
How do I know you'll actually launch?
Our founders have 40+ years combined industry experience. We have already registered the company, booked our first 5 trucks, and secured office space in Mumbai. Follow our progress on social media and check our launch timeline above for real-time updates.
What makes Koshiv different from established carriers?
We're building from scratch with: • Modern technology (MERN stack, React Native apps) • Transparent pricing (no hidden fees) • Customer-first culture (dedicated account managers) • Newer equipment (2024-2026 models) • Industry veterans who've seen what doesn't work.
Are you hiring drivers now?
Yes! We're accepting pre-applications for our launch team. Early applicants receive a ₹2,00,000 signing bonus and first pick of equipment. Apply today to secure your spot. Positions fill quickly.
What certifications do you have?
We're currently in the process of obtaining: • GSTIN Number (pending) • AEO Certification (application in progress) • Customs Bond (being secured) • BCAS Certified Cargo Screening (planned). All certifications will be complete before our Q2 launch.
What areas will you serve initially?
Phase 1 (Q2 2026): Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Western region • Phase 2 (Q3 2026): East coast ports, Bangladesh crossings • Phase 3 (Q4 2026): South coast ports, Nepal crossings. Check our coverage map above for details.
How can I become a partner?
We're actively recruiting: • Owner operators (lease purchase available) • Small fleet partners • Broker partners • Customs brokers. Fill out our partner interest form and we'll contact you within 48 hours.
Is this a real company or a college project?
Both! Koshiv Logistics is a registered company (Koshiv Logistics Pvt Ltd) founded by a 3rd year CSE student along with family members who have 40+ years of transport experience. The website and technology are being built by the founder himself, combining academic learning with real-world business.
