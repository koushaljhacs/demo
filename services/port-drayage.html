Service Overview

Port drayage is the short-distance transportation of containers between ports, rail ramps, and warehouses. Our service ensures your containers move efficiently through the supply chain's first and last mile.

Port to Warehouse

Efficient transportation of import containers from port terminals to your warehouse or distribution center.

Warehouse to Port

Timely delivery of export containers from your facility to the port for vessel loading.

Rail Ramp Transfer

Seamless movement of containers between rail ramps and warehouses for intermodal shipping.

Expedited Service

Priority handling for time-sensitive shipments with guaranteed delivery windows.

Heavy Haul Drayage

Specialized equipment for overweight and oversized containers requiring permits.

Appointment Scheduling

Coordination with port terminals for timely pickup and delivery appointments.

Key Features

  • GPS-tracked containers for real-time visibility
  • Chassis provisioning for all container sizes
  • Port terminal appointment management
  • Demurrage and detention monitoring
  • 24/7 dispatch and customer support
  • Electronic documentation and proof of delivery
  • Transloading services available
  • Container yard storage options

Why Choose Our Drayage Service

Speed & Efficiency

Quick turnaround times at ports minimize detention and demurrage charges.

Reliability

Consistent on-time pickup and delivery with real-time updates.

Port Expertise

Deep knowledge of port operations, gate procedures, and appointment systems.

Modern Fleet

Well-maintained trucks and chassis for reliable container transport.

Drayage Equipment

Day Cab Tractors

Maneuverable trucks optimized for port and short-haul operations.

Container Chassis

20', 40', 45', and 53' chassis for all container types.

Reefer Chassis

Generator sets for temperature-controlled container transport.

Heavy Haul

Specialized equipment for overweight and oversized containers.
