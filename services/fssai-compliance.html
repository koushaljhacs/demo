What is FSSAI Compliance?

The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) regulates the import and export of food products into and out of India. Compliance with FSSAI regulations is mandatory for all food businesses. Our planned service will help importers and exporters navigate these requirements. Details to be finalized closer to launch.

FSSAI License Assistance

Help with obtaining and renewing FSSAI licenses for food importers and exporters.

Clearance Support

Assistance during customs clearance for FSSAI-regulated food products.

Regulatory Updates

Information on changes to food safety regulations and standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FSSAI?

The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India is the regulatory body for food safety and standards in India.

Do I need FSSAI license for food import?

Yes, all food importers must have a valid FSSAI license and comply with food safety regulations.

What documents are required?

Typically required documents include commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, and test reports.

How long does FSSAI clearance take?

Clearance time varies depending on product type, documentation, and any required testing. We'll provide guidance once operational.

What products need FSSAI clearance?

All food products imported into India require FSSAI clearance, including processed foods, raw ingredients, and beverages.

Can you help with product testing?

Yes, we plan to coordinate with FSSAI-notified laboratories for required product testing services.
