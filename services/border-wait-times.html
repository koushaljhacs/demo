Service Overview

Border wait times can significantly impact your supply chain. Our real-time border wait time service provides up-to-date information on congestion levels at major India-Nepal and India-Bangladesh crossings, helping you plan routes, schedule drivers, and minimize delays.

Real-Time Updates

Live wait time information updated regularly from border checkpoints.

Multiple Crossings

Coverage of all major India-Nepal and India-Bangladesh border points.

Mobile Access

Access wait times on mobile devices for drivers and dispatchers on the go.

Alert System

Get notifications when wait times exceed thresholds or when conditions improve.

Historical Data

Access historical wait time patterns for better planning and forecasting.

Alternate Route Suggestions

Recommendations for alternative crossings when wait times are high.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often are wait times updated?

Wait times are updated every 15-30 minutes during operational hours, based on data availability.

Which borders are covered?

All major India-Nepal (Raxaul, Sunauli, Jogbani) and India-Bangladesh (Petrapole, Agartala, Changrabandha) crossings.

Is the service available 24/7?

Yes, wait times are updated 24/7, though data may be less frequent during nighttime hours at some borders.

Can I get alerts for specific borders?

Yes, you can set up custom alerts for wait time thresholds at specific border crossings.

Is historical data available?

Yes, we maintain historical wait time data for trend analysis and planning purposes.

Do you include holiday schedules?

Yes, we provide information on border holiday closures and their impact on wait times.
