Service Overview

Our Full Export Solution is designed to handle every aspect of your export process, from documentation to final loading. We are currently in the planning phase and will provide detailed information closer to our launch.

Export Documentation

Complete preparation and processing of all export documents including invoices, packing lists, and certificates of origin.

Shipping Bill Filing

Electronic filing of shipping bills with Indian customs for export shipments.

Container Loading

Professional loading and securing of cargo in export containers following international standards.

Export Packing

Specialized packing for export shipments to ensure safe transit and compliance with international standards.

Certificate of Origin

Assistance in obtaining certificates of origin for preferential tariff treatment under trade agreements.

Hazmat Export

Specialized handling, documentation, and transportation for hazardous materials export.

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for export?

Typical documents include commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, shipping bill, and certificate of origin.

How long does export processing take?

Processing time varies based on cargo type and destination. We'll provide timelines closer to launch.

Do you handle hazardous materials?

Yes, we plan to offer hazmat export services with proper documentation and handling procedures.

What is a shipping bill?

A shipping bill is the export declaration filed with customs, required for all export shipments.

Can you help with export packing?

Yes, we provide professional export packing services to ensure cargo safety during transit.

What is VGM certification?

Verified Gross Mass certification is required for all container exports as per SOLAS regulations.
