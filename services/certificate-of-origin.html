Certificate of Origin Services

Professional assistance with preferential and non-preferential certificates of origin for export shipments, helping you benefit from trade agreements and meet customs requirements.

Planned for Q2 2026

This page is under development. We are working on bringing you accurate information about our Certificate of Origin services.

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd is launching in 2026. Please check back later or contact us for specific inquiries.

Service Overview

A Certificate of Origin (COO) is a vital document that certifies the country of origin of exported goods. It determines eligibility for preferential duty rates under various trade agreements and is often required for customs clearance. Our service ensures accurate and timely issuance of both preferential and non-preferential certificates.

Preferential COO

Certificates for goods qualifying under Free Trade Agreements (FTAs) for reduced or zero duty rates.

Non-Preferential COO

Standard certificates for goods not covered by trade agreements, for general customs purposes.

Chamber Attestation

Assistance with chamber of commerce attestation and legalization of certificates.

Rules of Origin

Guidance on Rules of Origin criteria for various trade agreements and preference levels.

Declaration Preparation

Preparation of origin declarations and supporting documentation for COO applications.

Expedited Service

Fast-track processing for urgent shipments requiring immediate certificate issuance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Certificate of Origin?

A document certifying the country where exported goods were manufactured or produced, required for customs clearance in many countries.

What's the difference between preferential and non-preferential?

Preferential certificates qualify for reduced duties under trade agreements; non-preferential certificates are for general customs purposes only.

How long does certificate issuance take?

Standard processing takes 2-3 business days. Expedited service is available for urgent shipments.

Do all countries require Certificates of Origin?

Most countries require them for customs clearance, though requirements vary by destination and product type.

What is chamber attestation?

Chamber of Commerce attestation verifies the authenticity of the certificate and exporter's declaration.

Can certificates be issued electronically?

Yes, many countries now accept electronic certificates of origin (e-COO) for faster processing.
