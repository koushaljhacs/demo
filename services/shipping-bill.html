Shipping Bill Filing Services

Professional electronic filing of shipping bills with Indian customs for export shipments. Ensure compliance and avoid delays.

Planned for Q2 2026

This page is under development. We are working on bringing you accurate information about our Shipping Bill Filing services.

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd is launching in 2026. Please check back later or contact us for specific inquiries.

Service Overview

A shipping bill is the essential export document filed with Indian customs for all export shipments. Our service ensures accurate and timely filing through the ICEGATE portal, helping you avoid penalties and shipment delays. We are currently in the planning phase and will provide detailed information closer to our launch.

Electronic Filing

Direct filing of shipping bills through the ICEGATE customs portal with real-time tracking.

Document Verification

Thorough verification of all supporting documents before submission to customs.

Amendments

Assistance with amendments or corrections to filed shipping bills when needed.

Timely Submission

Ensure shipping bills are filed well before vessel cut-off times to avoid delays.

SB Number Tracking

Track your shipping bill status and receive updates on customs processing.

Query Resolution

Assistance in responding to any customs queries or requests for additional information.

Shipping Bill Filing Process

1

Document Collection

Gather all required documents including invoice, packing list, and export order.

2

Data Entry

Enter shipment details into the ICEGATE system with accurate information.

3

Document Upload

Upload supporting documents as required by customs regulations.

4

Assessment

Customs assessment and processing of the shipping bill.

5

Let Export Order

Receive LEO and permission to export after customs clearance.

6

SB Number Generation

Final shipping bill number issued for export documentation.

Required Information

  • Exporter details (IEC code)
  • Consignee information
  • Invoice value and currency
  • ITC-HS classification
  • Quantity and weight details
  • Port of loading and destination
  • Vessel name and voyage number
  • Export incentive claims (if any)

Why Choose Our Filing Service

Fast Processing

Quick turnaround time to meet vessel cut-off schedules and avoid demurrage.

Accuracy Guaranteed

Meticulous verification to prevent errors that could lead to penalties.

24/7 Filing Support

Round-the-clock assistance for urgent shipments and last-minute filings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a shipping bill?

A shipping bill is the primary export document filed with customs, containing details of the export shipment for clearance and documentation.

When should shipping bill be filed?

Shipping bills should be filed at least 24-48 hours before vessel loading to allow time for customs assessment.

What is IEC code?

Importer Exporter Code is a unique 10-digit number required for all import and export transactions in India.

Can shipping bills be amended?

Yes, amendments can be made post-filing, though certain restrictions apply. We can assist with amendment processes.

What is Let Export Order (LEO)?

LEO is the final customs approval allowing goods to be loaded onto the vessel for export.

How do I track shipping bill status?

Shipping bills can be tracked on the ICEGATE portal using the shipping bill number and date.
