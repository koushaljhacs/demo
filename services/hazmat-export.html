Hazmat Export Services

Professional hazardous materials export services with complete regulatory compliance, safe handling, and proper documentation for dangerous goods.

Planned for Q2 2026

This page is under development. We are working on bringing you accurate information about our Hazmat Export services.

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd is launching in 2026. Please check back later or contact us for specific inquiries.

Service Overview

Exporting hazardous materials requires specialized knowledge, strict regulatory compliance, and careful handling. Our hazmat export service ensures your dangerous goods are properly classified, packaged, documented, and transported in full compliance with international regulations including IATA DGR, IMDG Code, and ADR.

Classification

Proper classification of hazardous materials according to UN regulations and dangerous goods lists.

Documentation

Preparation of dangerous goods declarations, shipping papers, and safety data sheets.

Packaging

UN-certified packaging and proper packing instructions for all hazard classes.

Marking & Labeling

Correct hazard labels, UN numbers, and handling marks on all packages.

Compliance Review

Thorough review to ensure compliance with all applicable regulations.

Segregation

Proper segregation of incompatible hazardous materials during transport.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is considered hazardous material?

Any material that poses a risk during transport, including explosives, gases, flammable liquids, toxic substances, and corrosives.

What regulations apply to hazmat export?

International shipments must comply with IATA (air), IMDG (sea), and ADR (road) regulations based on transport mode.

Do you provide UN-certified packaging?

Yes, we can supply appropriate UN-certified packaging for various hazard classes and quantities.

What documentation is required?

Dangerous goods declaration, shipping papers, safety data sheets, and emergency response information.

Can you ship lithium batteries?

Yes, we handle lithium batteries and battery-powered devices in compliance with special provisions.

What training do your staff have?

Our team has specialized training in hazardous materials handling and regulatory compliance.
