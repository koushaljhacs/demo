Service Overview

Proper export packing is essential to protect your goods during international transit. Our export packing services ensure your cargo is securely packed using appropriate materials and techniques, meeting all international shipping standards and reducing the risk of damage.

Custom Crating

Custom-built wooden crates for machinery, equipment, and oversized items.

Palletizing

Professional palletizing of goods for efficient handling and transport.

Boxing & Cartoning

Secure boxing and cartoning for smaller items and consolidated shipments.

Fragile Handling

Specialized packing for fragile, delicate, and high-value items.

Waterproofing

Moisture barrier and waterproofing for protection against humidity and rain.

Security Sealing

Tamper-evident seals and security packaging for cargo protection.

Packing Materials

Wooden Crates

ISPM-15 certified heat-treated timber for international shipping

Corrugated Boxes

Heavy-duty cardboard boxes in various sizes

Bubble Wrap

Cushioning protection for fragile items

Stretch Wrap

Pallet wrapping and unitization

Foam Padding

Custom foam inserts for delicate equipment

Desiccants

Strapping

Labels

Shipping labels, handling instructions, and markings

Our Packing Process

1

Assessment

Evaluate cargo type, dimensions, weight, and fragility to determine packing requirements.

2

Material Selection

Choose appropriate packing materials based on cargo characteristics and destination.

3

Protective Wrapping

Apply bubble wrap, foam, or other cushioning materials to protect items.

4

Boxing/Crating

Place items in appropriate boxes or custom-built crates.

5

Void Fill

Fill empty spaces to prevent movement during transit.

6

Sealing & Labeling

Secure seals and apply shipping labels and handling instructions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ISPM-15 certification?

ISPM-15 is an international standard for wooden packaging material requiring heat treatment or fumigation.

How far in advance should I book packing?

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days before your shipment date for standard packing.

Do you pack hazardous materials?

Yes, we have trained personnel and appropriate materials for hazmat packaging.

Can you pack oversized items?

Yes, we specialize in custom crating for oversized and irregularly shaped items.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, all packing materials are included in our service. We use high-quality materials suitable for international shipping.

Can you pack at my location?

On-site packing can be arranged for larger projects. Contact us for details.
