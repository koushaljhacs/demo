Customs Clearance Services

Professional customs clearance assistance for import and export shipments. We handle documentation, duty calculation, and regulatory compliance.

Planned for Q2 2026

This page is under development. We are working on bringing you accurate information about our Customs Clearance services.

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd is launching in 2026. Please check back later or contact us for specific inquiries.

Service Overview

Our customs clearance services ensure your shipments move smoothly across borders. We handle all documentation and compliance requirements for both import and export shipments.

Documentation

Preparation and filing of all customs documentation including commercial invoices, packing lists, and certificates of origin.

Duty Calculation

Accurate calculation of import duties, taxes, and fees based on HSN codes and applicable trade agreements.

Compliance

Ensuring all shipments comply with Indian customs regulations and international trade laws.

Expedited Clearance

Priority processing to minimize delays and ensure your cargo moves quickly through customs.

Consultation

Expert advice on classification, valuation, and regulatory requirements for your specific products.

International Support

Coordination with customs brokers and authorities in other countries for seamless cross-border trade.

Key Features

  • ACE filing for US-bound shipments
  • ISF filing (10+2) compliance
  • HSN code classification assistance
  • Duty drawback documentation
  • Bonded warehouse transfers
  • FDA, USDA, and other agency clearances
