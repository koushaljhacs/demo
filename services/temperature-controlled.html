Temperature Controlled Logistics

Professional cold chain solutions for perishable goods, pharmaceuticals, and temperature-sensitive cargo. Real-time monitoring and reliable reefer transportation.

Planned for Q2 2026

This page is under development. We are working on bringing you accurate information about our Temperature Controlled Logistics services.

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd is launching in 2026. Please check back later or contact us for specific inquiries.

Service Overview

Temperature-sensitive cargo requires specialized handling and equipment to maintain product integrity throughout the supply chain. Our temperature-controlled logistics service provides reliable cold chain solutions with real-time monitoring, ensuring your perishable goods arrive in perfect condition.

Temperature Ranges

Deep Frozen

-25°C to -15°C

Ideal for frozen foods, ice cream, and long-term frozen storage.

Frozen

-15°C to 0°C

For frozen meats, seafood, and processed frozen products.

Chilled

0°C to 4°C

Fresh produce, dairy products, and chilled beverages.

Pharmaceutical

2°C to 8°C

Vaccines, biologics, and temperature-sensitive medicines.

Reefer Transportation

Modern refrigerated trucks with precise temperature control for door-to-door delivery.

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous temperature monitoring with alerts for any deviations.

Temperature Logs

Detailed temperature reports for compliance and quality assurance.

Cold Chain Packaging

Specialized packaging solutions for temperature-sensitive shipments.

Expedited Service

Priority handling for time and temperature-sensitive cargo.

GDP Compliance

Good Distribution Practice compliant for pharmaceutical logistics.

Real-Time Monitoring

Temperature Sensors

Multiple sensors throughout the trailer ensure consistent temperature across all cargo.

Remote Access

Monitor temperatures in real-time from your smartphone or computer.

Instant Alerts

Receive immediate notifications if temperatures deviate from set ranges.

Historical Data

Complete temperature history available for each shipment.

Compliance Reports

Generate temperature reports for regulatory compliance and audits.

Cloud Storage

All temperature data securely stored in the cloud for easy access.

Cold Chain Process

1

Pre-Cooling

Reefer unit pre-cooled to required temperature before loading.

2

Temperature Mapping

Verify temperature consistency throughout the trailer.

3

Loading

Quick loading to minimize temperature exposure.

4

Transit Monitoring

Continuous temperature tracking with real-time alerts.

5

Unloading

Temperature-controlled receiving at destination.

6

Documentation

Complete temperature records provided.

Cold Chain Features

  • Modern reefer fleet with advanced temperature control
  • Real-time temperature monitoring
  • Multi-temperature zone capabilities
  • Temperature data logging and reporting
  • GDP compliant processes
  • Backup refrigeration units
  • 24/7 monitoring and alert system
  • Trained cold chain specialists

Why Temperature Control Matters

Product Integrity

Maintain product quality and safety throughout the supply chain.

Regulatory Compliance

Meet strict requirements for pharmaceutical and food transportation.

Reduced Waste

Prevent spoilage and product loss with reliable temperature control.

Quality Assurance

Complete temperature documentation for quality audits.

Industries We Serve

Life Sciences

Laboratory samples, reagents, research materials

Floriculture

Fresh flowers, plants, nursery stock

Beverages

Wine, beer, juices, temperature-sensitive drinks

Chemicals

Temperature-sensitive industrial chemicals

Frequently Asked Questions

What temperature ranges do you offer?

We offer multiple temperature ranges from -25°C deep frozen to +25°C ambient, with precise control for pharmaceutical requirements.

How do you monitor temperature?

Real-time monitoring with sensors throughout the trailer, accessible via web portal with instant alerts for deviations.

Do you provide temperature records?

Yes, complete temperature logs are provided for every shipment, suitable for regulatory compliance and quality audits.

What happens if temperature deviates?

Instant alerts are sent, and our team takes immediate corrective action. All deviations are documented.

Do you handle pharmaceutical shipments?

Yes, we follow GDP guidelines and can accommodate pharmaceutical requirements including 2-8°C冷链.

What types of reefers do you have?

Modern reefer trailers with multi-temperature zones, backup systems, and advanced temperature control technology.
