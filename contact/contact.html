Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please use the emergency contact.

Can I visit your office?

Our Mumbai headquarters will open in Q2 2026. Visits are by appointment only. Please contact us in advance.

How do I apply for a driver position?

Visit our Drivers page or email careers@koshiv.com with your resume and CDL information.

Do you have a toll-free number?

A toll-free number will be available after our official launch in 2026. Currently, you can reach us via email.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Include your name, company, contact details, and a clear description of your needs. This helps us respond faster.

Is there a specific contact for international clients?

Our sales team handles all international inquiries. Email sales@koshiv.com for assistance.
