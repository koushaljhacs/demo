KEY HIGHLIGHTS

  • India's oldest operating port, established 1870
  • Only riverine major port in India (Hooghly River)
  • Two dock systems: Kolkata Dock & Haldia Dock Complex
  • Gateway port for Nepal, Bhutan, and North-East India
  • Multi-commodity: containers, bulk, liquid, project cargo
  • Close proximity to Petrapole (India-Bangladesh border)
  • Connected to Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor
  • Koshiv dedicated fleet positioned at Kolkata & Haldia
