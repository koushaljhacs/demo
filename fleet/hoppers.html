Hopper Trailers

Specialized trailers for dry bulk commodities including grains, cement, plastics, and other flowable materials. Available in pneumatic and gravity discharge configurations.

Equipment Overview

Our hopper trailer fleet is designed for efficient transport of dry bulk commodities. Available in both pneumatic and gravity discharge configurations, these trailers handle a wide range of materials from agricultural products to industrial powders.

Hopper Types

Pneumatic

1,500-1,800 cu ft

Pressurized discharge for powders and fine materials. Ideal for cement, fly ash, and plastic pellets.

Gravity Dump

1,600-2,000 cu ft

Bottom dump gates for free-flowing materials like grain, sand, and aggregates.

Combination

1,500-1,800 cu ft

Dual discharge capability for versatile material handling.

Discharge Systems

Pneumatic System

Uses air pressure to convey material through hoses to storage silos.

Discharge Rate: 500-2,000 lbs/min

Gravity Gates

Manual or air-operated gates for rapid unloading into pits or conveyors.

Discharge Time: 3-5 minutes

Auger System

Screw conveyor for controlled discharge in tight spaces.

Discharge Rate: 500-1,500 bushels/hr

Dimensions

Overall Length: 40-45 ft
Overall Width: 102 inches
Overall Height: 13 ft 6 inches
Number of Hoppers: 2-5 compartments

Capacity

Volume: 1,500-2,000 cu ft

Payload: 38,000-40,000 lbs

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between pneumatic and gravity hoppers?

Pneumatic hoppers use air pressure for discharge, ideal for powders. Gravity hoppers use bottom gates for free-flowing materials.

What materials can be transported in hoppers?

Dry, flowable materials including grains, cement, plastic pellets, sand, and various industrial powders.

How long does unloading take?

Gravity dump: 3-5 minutes. Pneumatic discharge: 30-60 minutes depending on distance and material.

Can different products be carried in one load?

Yes, multiple hopper compartments allow transport of different materials in a single trailer.

What is the typical capacity?

Hopper trailers typically carry 1,500-2,000 cubic feet or 38,000-40,000 pounds of material.

Are hopper trailers weather-resistant?

Yes, sealed hatches and covers protect cargo from rain and moisture during transport.
