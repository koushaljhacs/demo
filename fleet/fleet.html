Our Fleet

Information about equipment we are planning to acquire for our 2026 launch. All details are subject to change as we finalize our fleet planning.

Fleet acquisition is in planning phase. Specific quantities and models will be confirmed as we approach launch. Contact us for specific equipment inquiries.

Equipment Types

We are planning to offer the following equipment types upon launch. Click each for more details.

Dry Vans

Standard enclosed trailers for general freight. Planned sizes: 40' and 32' with air ride suspension and E-track.

  • Length: 40', 32'
  • Air ride suspension
  • E-track securement
View Dry Van Details Planned 2026

Refrigerated (Reefers)

Temperature-controlled trailers for frozen, chilled, and perishable cargo. Multi-temperature zones planned.

  • -25°C to 25°C
  • Multi-temperature zones
  • Remote monitoring
View Reefer Details Planned 2026

Flatbeds & Step Decks

Open deck trailers for oversized, heavy, or irregularly shaped cargo. Step deck options for taller loads.

  • Length: 40', 48'
  • Step deck available
  • Tarp systems
View Flatbed Details Planned 2026

Double Drop (RGN)

Removable gooseneck trailers for heavy equipment, machinery, and oversized loads requiring low deck height.

  • Deck height: 24"
  • Removable gooseneck
  • Capacity up to 60 tons
View RGN Details Planned 2026

Tankers

Stainless steel and lined tankers for liquid bulk transport including food grade and chemical products.

  • Food grade stainless
  • Chemical lined options
  • Insulated available
View Tanker Details Planned 2026

Hopper Trailers

Pneumatic and gravity dump trailers for dry bulk commodities like grains, cement, and plastics.

  • Pneumatic discharge
  • Gravity dump options
  • Aluminum or steel
View Hopper Details Planned 2026

Chassis (20'-40')

Container chassis for transporting shipping containers of various sizes from ports to destinations.

  • 20' to 40' adjustable
  • Tri-axle available
  • Slider configurations
View Chassis Details Planned 2026
