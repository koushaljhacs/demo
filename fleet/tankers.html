Tanker Trailers

Specialized tanker trailers for food-grade and chemical liquid bulk transport. Stainless steel and lined options available for various commodities.

Planned for Q3 2026

This page is under development. We are working on bringing you accurate information about our Tanker Trailer fleet.

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd is launching in 2026. Please check back later or contact us for specific inquiries.

Equipment Overview

Our tanker trailer fleet is designed for safe and efficient transport of liquid bulk commodities. Available in food-grade stainless steel and lined configurations for chemical products, with various compartment options for multiple product loads.

Tanker Types

Food Grade

6,000-8,000 gallons

Stainless steel construction for milk, edible oils, juices, and liquid food products.

Chemical

5,000-7,000 gallons

Lined carbon steel for acids, caustics, and industrial chemicals.

Petroleum

7,000-9,000 gallons

Carbon steel for fuels, lubricants, and petroleum products.

Material Options

Stainless Steel

304 and 316 grades for food-grade and corrosive products.

Milk, juices, wine, edible oils, pharmaceuticals

Lined Carbon Steel

Rubber or epoxy lined for chemical compatibility.

Acids, caustics, industrial chemicals

Carbon Steel

Standard carbon steel for non-corrosive products.

Fuels, lubricants, water, non-corrosive liquids

Technical Specifications

Dimensions

Overall Length: 42-48 ft
Overall Width: 102 inches
Overall Height: 13 ft 6 inches
Tank Diameter: 60-72 inches

Capacity

Gallons: 5,000-9,000 gallons
Liters: 19,000-34,000 liters
Weight Capacity: 45,000-48,000 lbs
Tare Weight: 10,000-12,000 lbs

Compartments

Number: 1-5 compartments
Manway Size: 20-22 inches
Outlet Size: 3-4 inches
Vapor Recovery: Optional

Construction

Material: Stainless/Carbon Steel
Insulation: 2-4 inches (optional)
Heating: Steam or electric (optional)
Suspension: Air ride or spring

Compartment Configurations

Single Compartment
Capacity: 7,000-9,000 gal
Use: Bulk liquids, one product
3 Compartments
Configuration: 2,500/2,500/2,500 gal
Use: Multiple products
5 Compartments
Configuration: 1,500/1,500/1,500/1,500/1,500 gal
Use: Split loads, multiple products

Key Features

Food Grade Certification

Stainless steel tankers with food-grade finishes for consumable products.

Chemical Lining

Corrosion-resistant linings for aggressive chemical transport.

Insulated Options

Temperature-controlled tankers for heat-sensitive products.

Vapor Recovery

Environmentally compliant vapor recovery systems.

Air Ride Suspension

Reduces product agitation during transport.

Steam Heating

Optional heating systems for viscous products.

Benefits of Tanker Trailers

Bulk Efficiency

Transport large volumes of liquid in a single load, reducing cost per gallon.

Product Purity

Dedicated food-grade equipment prevents contamination of consumable products.

Multi-Product Capability

Multiple compartments allow transport of different products in one load.

Environmental Compliance

Vapor recovery systems reduce emissions during loading/unloading.

Common Applications

  • Milk and dairy products
  • Edible oils and fats
  • Fruit juices and concentrates
  • Industrial chemicals and acids
  • Petroleum products and fuels
  • Liquid fertilizers
  • Pharmaceutical ingredients
  • Water and wastewater

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between food-grade and chemical tankers?

Food-grade tankers use stainless steel with polished finishes for easy cleaning, while chemical tankers may use lined carbon steel for corrosion resistance.

How are tankers cleaned between loads?

Tankers are cleaned using CIP (Clean-in-Place) systems or manual washing, depending on product requirements.

Can you transport multiple products in one load?

Yes, multi-compartment tankers can carry different products in separate compartments.

What is vapor recovery?

Vapor recovery systems capture fumes during loading/unloading to prevent environmental emissions.

Do you offer heated tankers?

Yes, insulated and heated tankers are available for products that require temperature control.

What certifications do food-grade tankers have?

Food-grade tankers meet FDA and relevant food safety standards for consumable products.
