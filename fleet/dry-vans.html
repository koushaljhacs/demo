Dry Vans

Standard and premium enclosed trailers for general freight transportation. Air ride suspension, E-track securement, and various door options available.

Planned for Q2 2026

This page is under development. We are working on bringing you accurate information about our Dry Van fleet equipment.

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd is launching in 2026. Please check back later or contact us for specific inquiries.

Equipment Overview

Our dry van fleet provides reliable, secure transportation for general freight. Available in both standard and premium configurations to meet various cargo requirements. All trailers are regularly maintained and equipped with modern securement systems.

Technical Specifications

Exterior Dimensions

Length: 53 ft (standard), 48 ft, 45 ft
Width: 102 inches (8.5 ft)
Height: 110 inches (9.2 ft)
Overall Height: 13 ft 6 inches

Interior Dimensions

Length: 52 ft 6 inches
Width: 100-101 inches
Height: 108-110 inches
Cubic Capacity: 3,489 cu ft (53 ft)

Weight Capacity

Max Gross Weight: 80,000 lbs (with tractor)
Tare Weight: 10,000-12,000 lbs
Payload Capacity: 44,000-45,000 lbs
Axle Capacity: 20,000 lbs per axle

Construction

Floor Type: Aluminum, hardwood (premium)
Side Walls: Aluminum sheet-and-post
Roof: One-piece aluminum
Suspension: Air ride (standard)

Standard vs Premium Models

Feature Standard Premium
Floor Type Aluminum Heavy-duty hardwood
Door Type Swing doors Roll-up doors
Securement E-track (4 rows) E-track + logistic posts
Ventilation 4 roof vents 6 roof vents with airflow
Interior Height 108 inches 110 inches
Floor Condition Standard Diamond plate threshold
Scuff Liner 12 inches 24 inches
LED Lighting Standard Premium with motion sensor

Key Features

Air Ride Suspension

Provides smoother ride, reduces cargo damage, and protects trailer components.

E-Track Securement

Multiple rows of E-track for flexible cargo securement with straps and bars.

Roof Vents

Ventilation system reduces condensation and helps maintain cargo quality.

LED Lighting

Bright, energy-efficient interior lighting for safe loading/unloading.

Roll-Up Doors

Easy operation, saves space at loading docks, available on premium models.

Heavy-Duty Floor

Hardwood floors in premium models resist forklift damage and wear.

Benefits of Dry Vans

Weather Protection

Fully enclosed design protects cargo from rain, snow, dust, and debris.

Security

Enclosed design and locking rear doors reduce theft risk during transit and parking.

Versatility

Suitable for a wide range of cargo including pallets, boxes, machinery, and more.

Aerodynamics

Smooth sides improve fuel efficiency compared to open trailers.

Common Applications

  • Palletized goods and general freight
  • Retail merchandise and consumer goods
  • Electronics and appliances
  • Furniture and home goods
  • Paper products and packaging materials
  • Automotive parts and components
  • Building materials and supplies
  • Machinery and equipment (non-Hazmat)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum payload for a dry van?

Typical payload capacity is 44,000-45,000 lbs depending on trailer weight and configuration.

What is the interior height of a dry van?

Standard interior height is 108-110 inches, sufficient for most palletized freight.

Do dry vans have temperature control?

No, dry vans are not temperature-controlled. For temperature-sensitive cargo, we offer reefers.

What securement options are available?

All trailers feature E-track systems compatible with straps, bars, and other cargo control devices.

Are the trailers equipped with liftgates?

Liftgates are available on select trailers by request. Please inquire when booking.

What is the difference between swing and roll-up doors?

Swing doors open outward; roll-up doors slide up into the trailer, saving space at docks.
