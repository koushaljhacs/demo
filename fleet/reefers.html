Refrigerated Trailers (Reefers)

Temperature-controlled trailers for frozen, chilled, and pharmaceutical cargo. Multi-temperature zones and real-time temperature monitoring available.

Planned for Q2 2026

This page is under development. We are working on bringing you accurate information about our Refrigerated Trailer fleet equipment.

Koshiv Technology and Logistics Pvt Ltd is launching in 2026. Please check back later or contact us for specific inquiries.

Equipment Overview

Our refrigerated trailer fleet provides reliable temperature-controlled transportation for perishable goods, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive cargo. All reefers are equipped with advanced refrigeration units and real-time temperature monitoring systems.

Temperature Ranges

Deep Frozen

-25°C to -15°C

Frozen foods, ice cream, seafood

Frozen

-15°C to 0°C

Frozen meats, vegetables, prepared foods

Chilled

0°C to 4°C

Fresh produce, dairy, beverages

Pharmaceutical

2°C to 8°C

Vaccines, biologics, medicines

Technical Specifications

Exterior Dimensions

Length: 53 ft, 48 ft, 45 ft
Width: 102 inches (8.5 ft)
Height: 110 inches (9.2 ft)
Overall Height: 13 ft 6 inches

Interior Dimensions

Length: 52 ft 6 inches
Width: 98-100 inches
Height: 105-108 inches
Cubic Capacity: 3,300-3,400 cu ft

Weight Capacity

Max Gross Weight: 80,000 lbs (with tractor)
Tare Weight: 12,000-14,000 lbs
Payload Capacity: 42,000-44,000 lbs
Axle Capacity: 20,000 lbs per axle

Insulation

Side Walls: 4 inches foam-in-place
Floor: 4 inches insulated
Roof: 4 inches foam
Front/Rear: 4 inches insulated

Refrigeration Units

Carrier Vector

Model: 8500, 1550
Capacity: 30,000-45,000 BTU
Multi-temperature zones, remote monitoring

ThermoKing

Model: SLXi, SLXe
Capacity: 28,000-42,000 BTU
Fuel-efficient, electric standby option

Carrier Transicold

Model: X4, X2
Capacity: 32,000-48,000 BTU
Advanced temperature control, telematics ready

Real-Time Monitoring

Temperature Sensors

Multiple sensors throughout trailer ensure consistent temperature across all cargo zones.

Remote Access

Monitor temperatures in real-time from any device with internet connection.

Instant Alerts

Immediate notifications if temperatures deviate from set parameters.

Temperature Logs

Complete temperature history available for compliance and quality assurance.

Compliance Reports

Generate reports for FDA, GDP, and other regulatory requirements.

Cloud Storage

All temperature data securely stored for easy access and retrieval.

Key Features

Multi-Temperature Zones

Divide trailer into up to 3 independently controlled temperature zones.

Pre-Trip Mode

Automated pre-cooling before loading ensures proper temperature at departure.

Electric Standby

Plug-in capability at warehouses and distribution centers saves fuel.

Air Distribution

Advanced air chutes ensure even temperature throughout the trailer.

Moisture Control

Automatic defrost cycles and humidity management prevent product damage.

Track System

Integrated cargo securement tracks for flexible loading configurations.

Benefits of Reefer Trailers

Product Integrity

Maintain precise temperature control to preserve cargo quality and safety.

Regulatory Compliance

Meet FDA, GDP, and other regulatory requirements with documented temperature records.

Extended Range

Fuel-efficient units provide extended range for long-haul temperature-controlled shipments.

Reduced Waste

Reliable temperature control prevents spoilage and product loss.

Common Applications

  • Frozen foods and ice cream
  • Fresh produce and dairy products
  • Pharmaceuticals and vaccines
  • Meat, poultry, and seafood
  • Floral and nursery products
  • Temperature-sensitive chemicals
  • Chocolate and confectionery
  • Wine and beverages

Frequently Asked Questions

What temperature range can reefers maintain?

Our reefers can maintain temperatures from -25°C to +25°C, suitable for frozen, chilled, and ambient cargo.

How long can a reefer run without refueling?

Typical run time is 80-100 hours depending on temperature settings and ambient conditions.

Do you offer multi-temperature trailers?

Yes, many of our reefers can be divided into multiple independently controlled temperature zones.

Can I monitor temperature during transit?

Yes, all our reefers feature real-time temperature monitoring accessible via web portal.

What is pre-cooling and why is it important?

Pre-cooling brings the trailer to the required temperature before loading, ensuring cargo safety from the start.

Do you provide temperature records?

Yes, complete temperature logs are provided for every shipment, suitable for regulatory compliance.
