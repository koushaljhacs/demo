NEPAL TRANSIT CARGO HUB

Raxaul is the primary transit point for Nepal's third-country trade under the Indo-Nepal Transit Treaty. Cargo from Kolkata Port and other Indian ports moves through Raxaul to Birgunj ICD and onwards to Kathmandu.

Customs bonded transit
Rail connectivity to Birgunj
Container handling facility
Bonded warehousing

Birgunj ICD (Inland Container Depot) is Nepal's largest dry port, located just 5 km from Raxaul border.
