ACT EAST POLICY & ASEAN CONNECTIVITY

Moreh is the cornerstone of India's Act East Policy, providing land connectivity to Myanmar and the ASEAN region. The India-Myanmar-Thailand Trilateral Highway, once completed, will link Moreh to Mae Sot in Thailand via Myanmar, opening up immense trade opportunities.

India-Myanmar-Thailand Highway
Kaladan Multi-Modal Project
ASEAN-India FTA
Border Trade Agreement (1994)

The trilateral highway will reduce travel time between India and Southeast Asia and boost trade volumes significantly.

BORDER HAAT - LOCAL TRADE

Moreh hosts a Border Haat (market) on Tuesdays and Fridays, allowing local communities from both sides to trade in traditional products. This fosters economic integration and cultural exchange.

Tuesday & Friday Local products Duty-free up to ₹10,000 Cultural exchange Traditional items Community trade
